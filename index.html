ABOUT

Virat Kohli was born on 5 November 1988 in Delhi, India. He is the son of Prem Kohli and Saroj Kohli. Virat is married to actress Anushka Sharma, and they have two children, Vamika and Akaay. Known for his exceptional batting skills, he is a right-handed batsman and former captain of the Indian cricket team. He also represents Royal Challengers Bengaluru (RCB) in the Indian Premier League. Throughout his career, Virat has received several prestigious awards, including the Arjuna Award, Padma Shri, and Khel Ratna Award, recognizing his outstanding contributions to Indian cricket.
